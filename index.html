Ye rahi teri routine

We will follow push, pull, leg workout routine which will take 3 days to complete a cycle.
By this way you can complete 2-cycles a week.

Day1(Push)


Pushups 3x10
Flat bench press 3x10
Smith Machine shoulder press 3x10
Dumbell Shoulder Press 3x10
Cable Triceps Push down 5x10

Day2(Pull)


Deadlift 3x10
Pull Down 4x10
Dumbell Shrugs 4x10
Preacher Curl 4x10

day3(Leg)



Warm Up
Body Weight Squats 3x10
Smith Machine Squats 4x10
Sumo Dumbell Squats 4x10
Leg Curl 4x10
